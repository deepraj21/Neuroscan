Brain Tumor Detection

The objective of this project is to develop a machine learning model that can accurately detect brain tumors in medical imaging data, such as MRI scans. By leveraging advanced image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms, the model aims to assist healthcare professionals in the early and accurate diagnosis of brain tumors, which can lead to timely treatment interventions and improved patient outcomes.
